## The 5 Decisions Before You Buy

Most container purchases stall because buyers get stuck on one of these questions. We've created guides to help you resolve each one.

### 1. What Size Do I Need?
Containers come in 20-foot and 40-foot lengths. The right choice depends on what you're storing and how much space you have.

**[Size Guide →]** | **[Size Calculator →]**

### 2. New or Used?
Used containers cost less but vary in condition. New containers cost more but arrive clean and dent-free. There's no wrong answer—just trade-offs.

**[Condition Guide →]**

### 3. Can It Be Delivered to My Property?
Container delivery requires specific access. Most rural properties work fine, but it helps to know the requirements upfront.

**[Delivery Guide →]**

### 4. What Should I Expect to Pay?
Prices vary by size, condition, location, and timing. We won't quote prices here, but we'll explain what drives them so you know what's reasonable.

**[Pricing Factors →]**

### 5. Do I Need a Permit?
Permit requirements depend on your location and intended use. Rural properties often have more flexibility, but it's worth checking.

**[Permits Overview →]**

## Why We Built This Site

Most container sellers jump straight to the sale. We think that's backwards.

If you understand your options first, you'll make a better decision—and we'll spend less time on quotes that don't fit. This site exists to help you figure out what you actually need before we talk pricing.

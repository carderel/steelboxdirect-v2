# Decision: Seller-Agnostic Positioning

**Date:** 2026-01-30
**Status:** Binding
**Decided by:** Stakeholder

---

## Decision

The site will be positioned as a **seller-agnostic** education and routing platform, not as a site for a specific contractor.

## Rationale

1. Stakeholder may sign with additional suppliers in the future
2. Neutral positioning increases trust
3. Provides optionality if current contractor relationship changes
4. Aligns with "education-first decision engine" strategy

## Implementation

### Do:
- Position as "authorized Freedom Conex contractor" (generic)
- Frame recommendations as routing to vetted suppliers
- Keep content focused on decisions, not seller identity
- Design quote flow to be supplier-agnostic

### Don't:
- Name Doug or specific contractor on public pages
- Create content tied to a single seller's identity
- Make claims that only apply to one contractor

## Quote Page Language

Instead of: "Get a quote from Doug"
Use: "Get a quote from an authorized supplier" or "Request pricing from a vetted contractor"

## Future Flexibility

This decision preserves:
- Ability to add multiple contractors
- Ability to change primary contractor
- Ability to sell/transfer the asset
- Clean separation between content and fulfillment
